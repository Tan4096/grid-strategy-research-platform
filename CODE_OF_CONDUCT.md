# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## Our standard / 基本原则

We want this repository to be welcoming, practical, and respectful.

We expect participants to:

- be respectful and constructive
- focus on the technical issue, not the person
- assume good intent
- welcome feedback and corrections
- avoid harassment, insults, or hostile behavior

## Unacceptable behavior / 不可接受行为

Examples include:

- personal attacks
- discriminatory or abusive language
- harassment or intimidation
- doxxing or sharing private information
- publishing real secrets or credentials in issues/PRs

## Enforcement / 执行方式

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this policy.
Repeated or severe violations may result in blocked participation.

## Reporting / 举报方式

If you need maintainer intervention, open a minimal public issue that only requests moderation follow-up.
Do **not** include private personal information, detailed accusations, real credentials, or exploit details in the public thread.

如果你需要维护者介入，请提交一个最小化的公开 issue，仅说明需要管理介入。
请**不要**在公开线程中披露私人信息、详细指控、真实凭证或可被滥用的细节。
